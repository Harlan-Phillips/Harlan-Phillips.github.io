About me

I am currently studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences at the University of California, Berkeley. With years of Python programming experience, I excel in data analysis, automation, and machine learning.

At Cornell University I'm developing a full-stack web application to classify transients using Flask, HTML, CSS, and SQL. I deployed this application on Amazon AWS EC2 and am currently working on a machine learning model to automate and streamline classifications.

My professional journey includes impactful internships at NASA, where I contributed to cutting-edge projects at NASA Ames Research Center. Additionally, I gained valuable experience working at the Bureau of Land Management, focusing on infrared and magnetic sensors. Other relevant experience include my role as a Tech Fellow at Codepath and a mentor at Curious Cardinals.
